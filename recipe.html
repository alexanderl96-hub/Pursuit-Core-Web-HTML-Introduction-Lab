The Best Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves — jelly or jam — spread on bread. The sandwich may be open-faced, or made of a single slice of bread folded over

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut on the left side
  3. On the other side, spread 1/8 inch of jelly
  4. Carefully place two halves together and with a knife, cut the sandwinchin half
